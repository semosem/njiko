Our vision

We promote a culture of harmony, better storytelling skills, diversity, and inclusivity. We want to help individuals and organizations develop the skills needed to build meaningful relationships, foster innovation, and create positive change in the world. Ultimately, our vision is to create the harmony that fosters productivity and empowers people through stories and experiences.

Njiko Narrate

This public speaking and storytelling workshop is designed to teach the art of public speaking, storytelling, and communication. Whether you're a student, teacher, or a professional, or just looking to build confidence, our modules will transform you into a captivating speaker. We teach how to infuse storytelling in public speaking to build anticipation and how to do active listening for better communication. We will work with you to create a storytelling strategy tailored to your business or individual needs. We will help you build the skills to infuse stories into your product and service designs, communicating your brand's values and message in a way that resonates with your audience. Njiko Narrate can bring your product to life by creating immersive storytelling that engages your customers and builds an emotional connection with your brand. We help you build story systems leave a lasting impression. We create synergy with you to gain a deep understanding of your organization and then structure it to fit your needs. Storytelling is essential to connect with their audience. Contact us today to learn how we can help you create a storytelling strategy, and build confidence in public speaking.

Njiko Diversity

This is a diversity, equality and inclusion workshop based on the diversity icebreaker model that helps individuals understand how and where they fit in a diversified group. Diversity and inclusion are essential for the success of any organization. The modules help develop team harmony and manage biases and assumptions. The workshop is designed to train participants on diversity and inclusion, and develop the skills necessary to manage a diverse team. Through interactive exercises participants learn how to communicate more effectively, build harmony, and collaborate across cultural and other differences. Contact us today to learn more about how Njiko’s diversity workshop can help your organization build a more diverse and inclusive team, and to schedule your free consultation.

Njiko Startup Pitch

Are you a startup with ideas but struggling to make the right impression on investors, potential co-founders, and customers? Njiko Startup Pitch is a pitching program designed to help startups like yours develop products that resonate with key stakeholders. Whether you are seeking investors or co-founders, learn how to craft impactful pitch with storytelling. We teach you the art of selling. Through a series of workshops and one-on-one coaching sessions, we teach you how to build storyboards that highlight your brand. We will train your team on how to create an impressive storytelling strategy that communicates your message. In addition to teaching expected value decisions and storytelling principles,our program includes hands-on training on how to prepare and deliver a compelling pitch that captures the attention of potential investors and partners. Contact us today to learn more about how Njiko Startup Pitch can help you turn your startup idea into a reality, and to schedule your free consultation

Our story

"Njiko" in Igbo denotes "harmony." The state of being in harmony with others or working together towards a common goal. We create value by strengthening diversity in teams. The Njiko concept highlights the significance of building a harmonic connection between organizations and individuals and enhancing communication by promoting storytelling skills. Contact Njiko now.
